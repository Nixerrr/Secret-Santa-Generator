How to use the Secret Santa Generator

The Secret Santa Generator randomly assigns a giftee to each person on the list and notifies them via email. Follow the following steps:

  1. Fill in the first row with your name and e-mail address.
  2. Use the and signs to add and remove rows.
  3. Fill in the rest of the rows with the names and e-mail addresses of the rest of the participants.
  4. Choose the Send me the results option if you want to get an extra email with all the results.
  5. Hit the Send emails! button.
